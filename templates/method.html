Data Sources

2.9 Gb of data were collected from various sources. Given the inconsistent naming conventions across datasets, geocoding and a technique inspired by species-to-species ortholog matching in biology (InParanoid) were used to correctly assign to correctly assign pricing and feature data to the correct locale.

Analysis

Pricing and selected features within the user-defined search area are retreived from the Postgresql database and real-time linear regressions (scikit-learn) performed to obtain expected pricing. The percentage differences between actual and expected pricing are then reported to identify over- and under-valued locales.
